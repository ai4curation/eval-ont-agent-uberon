## Rationale

The carotid body is a specialized chemoreceptive organ derived from neural crest cells and located near the bifurcation of the common carotid artery. While it sits in the adventitia of the artery, it is functionally and developmentally a component of the peripheral nervous system, not the cardiovascular system. PMID:32965908 supports this reclassification. The previous parentage was based on physical proximity rather than biological function.
