## Summary

This PR addresses issue #3409 by standardizing the relation used in Uberon-generated bridging axioms for life stage terms. Following the discussion in the issue thread, we now use **'in taxon' (RO:0002162)** instead of **'occurs in' (BFO:0000066)** for all life stage bridging axioms.

## Rationale

Previously, Uberon-generated bridges for externally maintained taxon-specific life stage ontologies (FBdv, WBls, HsapDv, MmusDv, SSLSO, etc.) used `occurs in` (BFO:0000066) in their equivalent class expressions, while the developmental-stage-ontologies repository used `part of` (BFO:0000050). As noted in the issue, this inconsistency across species was problematic. The discussion thread concluded with a consensus (notably from @cmungall and others) that `in taxon` (RO:0002162) is the appropriate relation for attaching life cycle/cycle stage terms to their taxa.
