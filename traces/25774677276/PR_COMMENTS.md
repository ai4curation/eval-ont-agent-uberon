# Fix incorrect part_of relationships for uterine tube infundibulum terms

## Summary
This PR addresses issue #2911 by removing incorrect anatomical relationships that linked uterine tube structures to a cardiac structure.

## Problem
Two terms were incorrectly annotated as having `part_of` relationships to conus arteriosus (UBERON:0003983), which is a cardiac structure (part of the right ventricle of the heart):
- UBERON:0007181 (serosa of infundibulum of uterine tube)
- UBERON:0007182 (muscle layer of infundibulum of uterine tube)

These terms are actually parts of the uterine tube infundibulum (UBERON:0003984), not the heart.

## Root Cause
The error likely arose from terminological confusion. "Infundibulum" is used as a synonym for both:
- The uterine tube infundibulum (UBERON:0003984) - the funnel-like part of the fallopian tube
- The outflow part of the right ventricle (UBERON:0005953) - also called the cardiac infundibulum

This led to incorrect part_of relationships being added to the cardiac conus arteriosus.
