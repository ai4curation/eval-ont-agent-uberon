## Background Research
The medial prefrontal cortex (mPFC) is a well-established brain region in neuroscience literature. It encompasses:
- Brodmann area 12 (UBERON:0013543)
- Brodmann area 25 (UBERON:0013556) 
- Brodmann area 32 (UBERON:0013560) - anterior cingulate cortex, dorsal
- Brodmann area 33 (UBERON:0006476) - anterior cingulate cortex, pregenual
- Brodmann area 24 (UBERON:0006101) - anterior cingulate cortex, ventral

Related terms already in UBERON:
- Anterior cingulate cortex (UBERON:0009835) - overlaps with mPFC
- Prelimbic area (UBERON:8440032) - rodent mPFC subregion
- Infralimbic area (UBERON:8440033) - rodent mPFC subregion
- Dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (UBERON:0009834) - sister region to mPFC
