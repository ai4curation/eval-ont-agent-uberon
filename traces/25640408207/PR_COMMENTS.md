# PR: Fix Issue #2911 - Remove Incorrect Part-of Relationships

## Summary

Fixed a long-standing anatomical error in the UBERON ontology where uterine structures were incorrectly linked to a cardiac structure.

## Problem

The terms UBERON:0007181 (serosa of infundibulum of uterine tube) and UBERON:0007182 (muscle layer of infundibulum of uterine tube) had incorrect `part_of` relationships pointing to UBERON:0003983 (conus arteriosus), a cardiac structure that is part of the right ventricle of the heart.

This error likely arose from confusion between two different meanings of "infundibulum":
- In cardiac anatomy: the outflow tract/cone (conus arteriosus) of the right ventricle
- In reproductive anatomy: the funnel-shaped distal end of the uterine tube

## Solution

Removed the incorrect `part_of UBERON:0003983` relationships from both terms. These uterine structures now correctly have only their anatomically appropriate relationship to UBERON:0003984 (uterine tube infundibulum), as expressed through their logical definitions.
