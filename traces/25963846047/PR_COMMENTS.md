## Problem

`carotid body` (UBERON:0001629) was previously asserted to be `part_of` `common carotid artery plus branches` (UBERON:0001530). This is anatomically incorrect: the carotid body is a chemoreceptive sensory organ located *in the adventitia of* the carotid bifurcation, i.e. adjacent to (not part of) the artery. The term's own textual definition already says it is "located near the bifurcation of the common carotid artery", and PMID:32965908 explicitly describes it as a sensory organ in the peripheral nervous system.
